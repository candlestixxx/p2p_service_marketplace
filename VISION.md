# VISION
The ultimate goal of ServiceHub is to create a seamless, two-sided marketplace (an "Uber for Services") that solves the coordination problem between service providers and clients.
Core foundational concepts:
1. Frictionless Discovery: Clients can easily find and filter local providers.
2. Real-time Availability: Appointments are instantly booked and guaranteed not to overlap.
3. Automated Monetization: Providers monetize their services instantly via Stripe Connect, and the platform retains a minor fee.
